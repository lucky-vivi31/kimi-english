Hi! My name is Chloe, and I have the most adorable pet in the world – a tiny golden hamster named Mochi! Mochi has soft, fluffy fur and big, round black eyes that look like little buttons. When I first got Mochi, I was so excited that I wanted to hold him right away. But my mom reminded me to be gentle because small animals can feel frightened easily. That was the beginning of my journey in learning how to love and care for a living creature. 

Every morning, I check on Mochi and make sure he has fresh water and food. Hamsters are fascinating little animals! Did you know that they can store food inside their cheek pouches? Their cheeks can stretch to almost the size of their body! Mochi also loves to run on his little wheel at night because hamsters are nocturnal – that means they are most active when it is dark outside. I enjoy watching him explore his little home with so much energy and curiosity! 

Last week, something worrying happened. When I checked on Mochi in the morning, he was sitting very still in the corner of his cage. He refused to eat or drink. I felt very worried and my heart ached for him. I gently stroked his soft fur and spoke to him in a calm, soft voice. By the afternoon, he started moving around again and nibbled on some food. I was so relieved! That day, I truly understood that loving an animal means being gentle and patient even when you are worried. 

Having Mochi in my life has taught me so much about love and responsibility. A pet depends on us completely. Every day, I try to be the kindest and gentlest owner Mochi could ever have. When I hold him carefully in my hands, I feel such warmth and happiness inside. 

In the future, I will continue to take the best care of Mochi. I will also read more books about hamsters so I can understand him even better. What about you? Do you have a pet? How do you show love and gentleness to the animals around you?
