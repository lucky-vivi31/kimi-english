Every clear night, I go outside with my telescope. I enjoy learning about different constellations and planets. Did you know that stars twinkle because of Earth's atmosphere? The moving air makes the starlight appear to dance! I also love finding the Big Dipper and the Little Dipper. These patterns in the sky have been guiding travelers for thousands of years. Learning about stars brings me so much happiness!

Last summer, something incredible happened! My family and I traveled to the mountains where the sky was very dark and clear. One night, we witnessed a magnificent meteor shower! Hundreds of shooting stars streaked across the sky like sparkling diamonds. I made three wishes on the shooting stars. That magical night reminded me how wonderful and peaceful nature can be.

Now, whenever I look at the stars, I feel calm and joyful at the same time. The night sky teaches me that there is so much to discover and explore in our amazing universe. In the future, I will continue to study the stars and planets. Maybe one day, I will become an astronomer and help other people learn about the wonders of space!
